About us

Khan foundation established at 12 December, 2020. It is a non-government organization that is founded to help unemployed, poor, and rural area people. Members of this organization can deposit money for their future and they will get back their investment with interest. The authority of this organization will invest money in a business that comes from members and the member will get profit from the earning of this business. To get this opportunity members must have to maintain some rules and regulation. 20 % of the earnings of this organization will be given to poor people. And also, this organization will collect jakat fund to provide young people who is deprived of education.
